Welcome to Easy Buy with 3D Augmented Reality

Transform your home decor shopping experience with augmented reality.

Abstract

The "Easy Buy with 3D Augmented Reality" app is designed to make shopping for home decor easier and more fun. You can sign up, browse through different home decor items, and see how they would look in your home using augmented reality. The app also lets you add items to your cart, pay securely, and track your orders.

Introduction

Imagine being able to browse through a wide selection of home decor items from the comfort of your own home. With our app, you can do just that! Whether you're looking for furniture, lighting, or decorative accessories, our comprehensive product catalog has something for everyone.

We take your shopping experience to the next level with augmented reality (AR) visualization. With just a tap, you can see how a piece of furniture or a decorative item would look in your own living space. It's like trying before you buy, right from your smartphone or tablet!

Our secure checkout and payment processing ensure that your transactions are safe and protected. You can track your orders every step of the way and leave reviews to help other shoppers make informed decisions.

Behind the scenes, our app is powered by advanced AI technology that enhances the visual clarity of product images by removing distracting elements. Plus, our admin dashboard makes it easy for us to manage the app and ensure that everything runs smoothly.

Modules

  • Module 1: User Authentication and Registration

    Users can securely create accounts, log in, and access the app's features.

  • Module 2: Product Management

    Admins can manage the product inventory, update product information, and track stock levels.

  • Module 3: 3D Augmented Reality

    Users can visualize home decor items in their real environment using AR technology.

  • Module 4: Order Management

    Users can track orders, view order history, and receive order updates.

  • Module 5: User Reviews and Ratings

    Users can leave reviews and ratings for home decor items.

  • Module 6: Admin Dashboard

    Admins have a centralized platform for managing products, orders, users, and more.

  • Module 7: Chat System Integration

    Users can communicate with sellers and customer support through chat.

  • Module 8: Payment Gateway Integration (Stripe)

    Secure online payments are processed through the Stripe payment gateway.

  • Module 9: Email and Notification Services

    Users receive updates and notifications about orders and promotions.

  • Module 10: Background Removal through AI

    AI technology removes backgrounds from product images for better visual clarity.

  • Module 11: Recommendation System With AI

    AI algorithms provide personalized product recommendations to users.
